Chess Voice Control

Ready

How to use:

  1. Press 'V' to activate voice recognition
  2. Speak a chess square (e.g., "e4" or "e four")
  3. The piece will move to the spoken square

Example commands:

  • "e4"
  • "move to e four"
  • "d five"
  • "cancel" (to stop)

Press 'V' anytime to start voice recognition
